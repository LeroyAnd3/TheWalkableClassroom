Hello and welcome to our Concentration Game!

Here are some instructions on how to play:

Foremost, make sure you are 1) logged in and 2) you have made a deck of cards (i.e. your terms and their hints)

Playing the game:
1. Select a board configuration from the drop down menu. This specifies the size (meaning number of cards) of your deck to play.
2. Next select a card background. Instead of plain, white there are several other options for you to choose form.
3. Then, press play.
